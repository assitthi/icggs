Department of Geography, Faculty of Social Sciences, Srinakharinwirot University and Can Tho University would like to invite you to the 3nd International Conference on Geography and Geoinformatics (ICGGS). The conference will be held on 21-22 December 2023 in Can Tho, Vietnam. ICGGS aims at providing a platform for scholars around the world to engage in broad spectrum of issues ranging from Natural resources &environment, Socioeconomic, Geoinformatics application and Education, followed by exploring the growth of technological advance in Geoinformatics and its impact on planning and solving global problems.
